Welcome to our PiCar!

Two mode of operations:
  • In the autonomous mode, the piCar can be regarded as a search and find embedded robot whose goal is to identify threats or missing items. With very limited compute from the RPi and less energy use, this is a great embedded project that can easily help find items or examine a dangerous environment from a distance.
  • In the controlled mode, the user can easily make sure that they control the robot even when they are not directly next to it, giving the user more flexibility.
